üë©‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

  1. Prepare the Patties:
    • In a bowl, mix ground beef with onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
    • Shape into 4 equal patties (slightly larger than the buns, as they shrink when cooking).
  1. Cook the Patties:
    • Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat.
    • Cook patties for 3-4 minutes on each side (medium), or longer if you prefer well-done.
    • Place cheese slices on top in the last minute to melt.
  1. Toast the Buns:
    • Lightly toast hamburger buns on the grill or pan for extra flavor.
  1. Assemble the Burgers:
    • Spread sauces (ketchup, mustard, mayo) on buns. Layer lettuce, tomato, onions, patty with melted cheese, and pickles.
  1. Serve:
    • Top with the other half of the bun. Serve hot with fries and a cold drink!
